# Athar Herb - E-commerce Platform

## Project Overview
Athar Herb is a comprehensive e-commerce platform built with Laravel 12 and Filament admin panel, designed for selling herbal products and natural remedies. The platform combines modern web technologies with a robust content management system to deliver a complete online shopping experience.

## Key Features & Capabilities

### E-commerce Core
- **Product Management**: Complete catalog system with categories, SKUs, stock tracking, and pricing
- **Order Processing**: Full order lifecycle from cart to delivery with status tracking
- **User Management**: Customer accounts with role-based access (customer/admin)
- **Inventory Control**: Stock quantity monitoring with low-stock alerts and expiration date tracking
- **Review System**: Customer product reviews with rating and approval workflow

### Content Management
- **Dynamic Pages**: Custom page creation with SEO optimization
- **Blog System**: Post management with featured images and publishing controls
- **Testimonials**: Customer testimonial showcase with approval system
- **Banner Management**: Homepage and promotional banner system with link management
- **FAQ System**: Organized frequently asked questions with sorting

### Advanced Features
- **Wishlist Functionality**: Save products for later purchase
- **Multi-category Products**: Products can belong to multiple categories
- **Image Management**: Primary and secondary product images with file handling
- **SEO Optimization**: Meta titles and descriptions for all content
- **Admin Dashboard**: Filament-powered administrative interface

## Target Users
- **Customers**: Health-conscious consumers seeking natural herbal products
- **Administrators**: Store managers handling inventory, orders, and content
- **Content Managers**: Staff responsible for blog posts, testimonials, and site content

## Business Value
- Streamlined herbal product sales with specialized inventory tracking
- Professional admin interface for efficient store management
- SEO-optimized content system for better search visibility
- Scalable architecture supporting business growth
